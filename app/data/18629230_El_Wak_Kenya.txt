with Somalia. ==Location== The town is located in Mandera County, in extreme north eastern Kenya, approximately , by road, northeast of Wajir, the nearest large town. El Wak, Kenya is approximately , by road southwest of the town of Mandera, where the county headquarters are located. The coordinates of the town are: 2°48'10.0"N, 40°55'39.0"E (Latitude:2.802771; Longitude:40.927510). ==Overview== The town, which lies approximately halfway between Wajir and Mandera, is the southern end of the proposed Elwak–Mandera Road. Directly across the border from El Wak is the neighbourhood in Somalia called Bur Ache, in the greater El Wak, Somalia area.
