Ellakkala (, ) is a suburb close to Nittambuwa in Gampaha Administrative District. It is located about northeast of Nittambuwa. It is located on the Colombo Kandy A1 highway approximately from the Pasyala Junction. Most Ellakkala lands are within Attanagalle Pradeshiya Sabha while some parts within Mirigama Pradeshiya Sabha. Specially lands are between Ellakkala and Meevitiya. Adjoining suburbs are Haggalla, Walaliyedda, Godagama, Meevitiya, Katakaalapitiya, Allalamulla and Ma-Imbula. == Attractions == Traditional Pattini Dewol is a famous site in December. There are many parades.
